Best Friend (2023) Tubi Movie Cast and Review

Publish date: 2024-07-26

Best Friend 2023 Tubi cast includes Serayah, Blue Kimbal and Wesley Johnathan in lead roles. The thriller movie releases on 24 February, 2023.

The movie is written and directed by Chris Stokes.  The movie is about two people being best friends since grade school who never saw each other in a romantic way.

The movie beings when one starts to feel jealous after his friend starts to date another guy.

The lead role is played by Serayah as Trisha and Jamie by Blue Kimble.

The movie is about 1 hour and 44 minutes long and is premiering on Tubi TV.

Meet The Cast Of Best Friend 2023 On Tubi

Best Friend Tubi cast consists of Serayah, Blue Kimble, Wesley Johnathan and Mike Hill. The movie also has other additional cast members.

Serayah As Trisha

Serayah plays Trisha in the movie who falls in love with the guy William.

She got her fame from the television series “Empire” as Tiana Brown who is a singer.

In an interview with enterprises, she shared that before Empire she used to do a lot of modelling, print jobs and give an audition.

She is not just an actress and model but a singer and getting her first role as a singer helped her get recognized as a singer.

The movie also gave her a chance to work with Taylor Swift in her music video “Bad Blood” as a character called “Dilemma”.

Blue Kimble As Jamie

Blue Kimble plays Jamie. Jamie is Trisha’s best friend who gets jealous after she starts dating another guy.

Before trying for the film industry, he was a professional football player but found passion in acting and pursued it as a career.

In an interview with the chilled magazine, he shared that he has a very active lifestyle, usually works out in his spare time and spends as much time with his family.

Previously he was a part of the NFL as he played for the Buffalo Bills. Blue also appeared in the movie “Fast and Furious 5” as a guest actor which aired in 2011.

Wesley Jonathan As Derrick

Wesley plays Derrick in the new Tubi movie. Wesley started his acting career in 1990 and is known for his role in the sitcom “What I Like About You” as Gray Thorpe.

As per IMDB, he has worked in the movies like “Bad Dad Rehab” as Shawn, “A Boy. A Girl. A Dream.” as Wesley, “Trigger” as Collin Mass and “The Men In My Life” as Sean Helms.

He was born in Los Angeles and also lived in Germany for four years when he was young. He showed interest in acting after attending his cousin’s audition when he was six.

Mike Hill As Kelson

Mike Hill is seen playing Kelson in Tubi’s movie. Mike is a well-known television personality and talk show host and also a producer, writer and actor.

He is also known for his work in the movies “The Bright Path – The Johnny Bright Story”, “Not Alone” and “Best Friend”.

At the beginning of his career, he worked as a  sports director at WHAG-TV in 1995.

He also joined ESPN and worked there for nine years. He hosted the show “America’s Pregame” in 2014, but it was cancelled in 2015.

Mike also co-hosted the morning show with Sharon Reed aired on the News Channel “Start Your Day With Sharon and Mike”.
