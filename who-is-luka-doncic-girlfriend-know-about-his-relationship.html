Is Luka Doncic Going To Propose His Longtime Girlfriend, Anamaria Goltes For Marriage?

Publish date: 2024-09-10

Slovenian national basketball player, Luka Doncic came into limelight after the Atlanta Hawks selected him in the 2018 NBA draft. The two times NBA All-Star made his country proud at the age of 17 when he won the first-ever European Basketball Championship title.

Due to Dallas Mavericks point guard Doncic's height of popularity, many of his fans are questioning who is his perfect match? If you love to read more about his personal life then, read the article to the end.

Who is Luka Doncic's Girlfriend?

22-years-old, Doncic's girlfriend is none other than Anamaria Goltes, who is a 23 years old lingerie model. She is from Zagorje ob Savi, Slovenia, but currently based in Dallas, Texas.

Talking about Doncic's longtime beau, Anamaria is working under a Slovenian model management company, Lisca. She is currently serving as the ambassador of the Lisca's Cheek product lines.

Anamaria is also embracing her uniqueness as a fashion and lifestyle content creator. On a similar hand, famous social media influencer, Noen Eubanks is fond of making random comedy and musical videos on TikTok.

How the Couple Met for the First Time?

6 feet 7 inches tall, Doncic and his girlfriend, Anamaria had their first meeting on the seashore of Croatia in 2011. At that time, the duo were just 12 years old and started liking one another shortly after a while.

Luka shared an adorable picture of him with Anamaria of their young teenage period via Instagram on 14th July 2018. In the photo's caption, he wrote through back of 2011 and tagged her.

Doncic's biggest supporter and no. 1 fan, Anamaria always stayed on his side. She cheers for him whenever he feels low and self-proclaimed herself in the #TeamLuka.

Both are Avid Animal Lovers & Fond of Traveling...

Former Real Madrid Baloncesto small forward, Doncic is raising two pet dogs. Furthermore, their names are Hugo the Pomerian and Gia, a Snow-white Huskey mixed-breed. Currently, they are residing in Dallas, Texas with their doggies.

Do you know? The power couple is avid travelers who seeks new adventures to explore. Back in November 2019, they went to Greece for their summer vacation. Besides, they often goes for marathons and sometimes watches Netflix. 

Is Luka Planning to Propose his Longtime Beau, Anamaria?

Yes, the Dallas Mavericks defensive guard Luka is planning to his love of life, Anamaria from a very long time. In an interview with Heavy, he proclaimed that he is going to surprise his longtime beau with a beautiful proposal one day.

Nonetheless, it seems like Luka's beloved mom, Mirjam Poterbin, estranged wife of Sasa Doncic, might be against their love story. It's all started when his mother stopped following his girlfriend on Instagram and untagged her in her posts in early-2018.

A few months later, Doncic's mother reconciled with his longtime partner, Anamaria after finding her good for her son in July 2018. Since then, they are living happily ever after without any problems.
