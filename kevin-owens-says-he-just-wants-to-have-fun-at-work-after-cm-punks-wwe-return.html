Kevin Owens Says He Just Wants to Have Fun At Work After CM Punks WWE Return

Publish date: 2024-09-23

CM Punk’s return to WWE at Survivor Series marked one of the biggest surprises in WWE history. The company decided to follow it up by having Punk cut a promo on RAW for the first time in a decade and now Kevin Owens finally commented on Punk returning to WWE.

This week’s episode of Monday Night RAW had a lot of things going for it, especially considering both Randy Orton and CM Punk were present. CM Punk cut a promo for the first time in almost a decade and it received mixed responses from fans.

Fans and pro wrestlers alike are still trying to process that The Second City Saint is actually back in WWE after all these years. While speaking with James Williams, Kevin Owens talked about CM Punk’s return. Owens simply noted that he wanted to have fun at work, so if Punk has that same mindset, then all is good.

“Um, I just want to have fun at work. So if he’s got that mindset, great.”

CM Punk’s first major feud will be with familiar foe. Ringside News exclusively reported what brand CM Punk will be part of as well. Even Shawn Michaels sees great things in Punk’s WWE future, so we’ll have to see how it goes with Punk.
