Who Is Viviana Rossi From AGT? Stunning Spanish Aerial Artist Gets Four Yes From The Judges

Publish date: 2024-07-11

Viviana Rossi is the new star of America’s Got Talent as her sensual aerial performance heated the AGT platform and fans this week.

This week’s audition rounds welcomed the Spanish beauty, Viviana Rossi.

Everyone was stunned the moment she walked in and went on to advance to the next round as well.

This is not the first time Rossi has performed on a big stage, as she has previously been a part of Italy’s ‘Tu Si Que Vales’ in 2019, making it one of her most memorable moments.

Viviana Rossi Is A Spanish Aerial Artist Shining Through AGT Platform

Viviana Rossi introduced herself as a professional aerial performer from Spain on the stage of the AGT.

She impressed the judge panel and audience, who cheered on her from the start. Rossi has been to many countries and performed there, making it her accomplishment.

Likewise, the Spanish Aerial artist had always wanted to perform on an American stage; thus, she could not find a better platform other than AGT.

Rossi also revealed that her AGT performance is her comeback to the stage after being injured onstage, some time ago.

Judge Simon Cowell called the performance original as Rossi’s performance included amazing aerial stunts with water and a bathtub. As expected, the beautiful and talented Rossi walked away from the stage with four yeses.

Talent Recap has named her performance a unique aerialist.

How Old Is Viviana Rossi?

Viviana Rossi is a young woman, who is flaunting her beautiful aerial stunts at the age of 26. She was born on February 16, 1996, in Barcelona, Spain.

Rossi is undoubtedly an astonishing female who inspires other women to be as fearless as her and overcome every injury and obstacle to meet one’s dreams.

Her grace as a sultry woman on stage has garnered all the eyeballs, while her aerial talent adds a cherry on the top.

Meet Viviana Rossi Family

Viviana Rossi hails from an Italian family whose roots are infused with circus acts. According to Viaggi News, her family has been a part of the circus for generations.

Her grandfather was a magician, while her father Enni Rossi is a trainer in the circus. Later on, her mother Gloria Jiminez joined their line as entertainers, after her marriage with Enni.

Thus, the AGT star contestant had always been a part of extraordinary while she came to excel in aerial numbers with particular rims and belts.

She is currently affiliated with Cirque du Soleil, an entertainment company, and makes a living out of her profession. The Italian-Spanish Rossi family is proud of her AGT results back in Spain.

Follow Viviana Rossi On Instagram

The versatile Viviana Ross has acquired more than 20k followers on Instagram after her audition clip hit the web.

According to her Instagram bio, she is also a professional athlete, a gymnastics champion, a world traveler, a nutritionist, and a trainer.

Her amazing onstage pictures on the feed have garnered thousands of likes and comments in a short while.

She is currently performing in France but will relocate to the U.S. for further AFT performances.
