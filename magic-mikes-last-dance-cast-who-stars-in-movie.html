Magic Mike's Last Dance cast: Who stars in the movie?

Publish date: 2024-08-25

MAGIC Mike first premiered 10 years ago in 2012, launching actor Channing Tatum to superstardom.

Following Magic Mike XXL, the final installment in the film franchise will be Magic Mike's Last Dance.

Who stars in Magic Mike's Last Dance?

On April 13, 2022, Deadline announced some of the cast that will star in Magic Mike's Last Dance.

Salma Hayek, 55, is set to star in the series, which is a new face for this film franchise.

The full talent list has yet to be announced as the project is still in its early phases of production.

Deadline also reports that Steven Soderbergh, who directed the first film will return to direct the final picture.

It is likely that Channing Tatum, 41, will return for his iconic role as Magic Mike.

Gregory Jacobs, who directed the 2015 film Magic Mike XXL, will return to produce alongside Reid Carolin, Nick Wechsler, and Peter Kiernan.

When does Magic Mike's Last Dance premiere?

The film's release date has not yet been announced by the studio.

The outlet reports that it is currently in production in London and will premiere on HBO Max.

Magic Mike and Magic Mike XXL grossed nearly $300million combined worldwide.

In 2017 two films also inspired the on-stage production Magic Mike Live.

The first film is currently available to rent on YouTube, Apple TV, and other platforms.

Some of the actors in the first film include Joe Manganiello, Matt Bomer, Alex Pettyfer, and Matthew McConaughey.

What is Magic Mike about?

The first film is about an experienced male stripper teaching a newcomer how to be a part of the industry.

The IMDb description of the film reads: "A male stripper teaches a younger performer how to party, pick up women, and make easy money."

Mike Lane, played by Tatum is a roofer by day and stripper by night.

He ends up being the star of the Kings of Tampa, a group of male strippers in Tampa, Florida.
