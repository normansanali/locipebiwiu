Billy Ray Cyrus is tying the knot once more. The country singer started dating singer Firerose in 2022 after divorcing his longtime wife Tish Cyrus.

Who is Billy Ray Cyrus’ girlfriend Firerose?

As a singer-songwriter like Billy, Firerose claims on her website that she has had a “lifelong passion for songwriting” since she was a small child.

The Newtown School for the Performing Arts, which is regarded as one of Australia’s “top Performing and Visual Arts schools,” is where Firerose studied while she lived in Sydney.

Billy revealed to PEOPLE on Nov. 16, 2022, that he and Firerose were engaged after a few months of rumors. Cyrus said, “She’s the real deal. Billy and Firerose had posted a number of selfies on Instagram around a month earlier.
