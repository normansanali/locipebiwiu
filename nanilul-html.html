Publish date: 2024-09-23

Twitch gamer and IRL broadcaster who has accumulated more than 120,000 followers playing games like Hearthstone, The Walking Dead, and Playerunknown's Battlegrounds. Her username is nani. 

Before Fame

She studied communication science, history, and philosophy at Abitur and graduated in 2013. 

Trivia

She has a self-titled YouTube channel where she has posted baking videos, gaming videos, and vlogs.

Originally from Germany, she later settled in Stockholm, Sweden. Her boyfriend has appeared in numerous photos on her Instagram account.
