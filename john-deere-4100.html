John Deere 4100 Tractor Price, Specs, Review, Serial Numbers, Horsepower, Weight, Engine Oil Capacity, History & Pictures

John Deere 4100 is one of the most recent tractor models built by John Deere. The production started in 1998 and continued till 2001.

The John Deere and Yanmar association is one of the most trusted regarding tractor and engine making.

The John Deere 4100 tractor is equipped with a Yanmar 3TNE74 diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled engine, which produces 20HP at rated rpm 2650. The coolant capacity is 2.4 liters.

The John Deere 4100 tractor is fitted with  8 forward and 4 reverse gears. It has got an oil capacity of more than 8 liters.

John Deere 4100 is rated to be one of the lightest farming tractors in terms of its weight. The John Deere 4100 tractor was available in a Narrow version, which could not be provided with a mid-PTO.
