How old was Kristen Stewart in Twilight?

Publish date: 2024-08-23

KRISTEN Stewart has been acting since she was 9 years old and is set to star in the upcoming film Spencer at the age of 31.

After starting in 1999, Stewart didn't become a household name until she landed the role of Bella Swan in Twilight.

How old was Kristen Stewart in Twilight?

In The Twilight Saga, there were five movies released between 2008-2012.

The first movie, Twilight, was released on November 21, 2008, and at the time, Stewart was only 18 years old.

Born April 9, 1990, Stewart's fame skyrocketed following the release of that film.

Stewarts age during the sequel films was:

  • The Twilight Saga: New Moon (19)
  • Twilight Saga: Eclipse (20)
  • Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn Pt. 1 (21)
  • Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn Pt. 2 (22)

Following the release of the last movie in 2012, at just 22-years-old, Stewart became the world's highest-paid actress.

Was Kristen Stewart nominated for an Oscar?

While Stewart has dozens of awards to her name, she is looking to add an Oscar to her growing list as she portrays Princess Diana in the upcoming film Spencer.

For many years, Stewart has mostly been defined by her Twilight success. Forbes reported that of the $4 billion Stewart's 25 films have earned worldwide, 93% of it has come from The Twilight Saga.

Despite being tied to the saga, Stewart hopes to change that with Spencer, as it has already thrown her into the Oscar circle following the release of the first footage at CinemaCon.

"I just saw the teaser trailer for #Spencer & a 5 minute clip from the film," Film critic Scott Menzel said in a tweet. "Based on the footage, it looks like Kristen Stewart will be getting an Oscar nomination for her performance as Diana. I'm feeling very confident that this is going to be #KristenStewart's big moment."

Journalist Jason Guerrasio added in, "Just saw footage of SPENCER. Kristen Stewart is going all out for that Oscar glory. And her Princess Di accent is spot on! #CinemaCon."

Who is Kristen Stewart engaged to?

On November 2, 2021, Kristen confirmed to Howard Stern on the Howard Stern Show that Dylan Meyer had proposed to her.

Dylan is an actress, producer, and writer.

She is known for writing credits in films like Loose Ends and XOXO. 

Dylan also worked on the TV series Miss 2059 and most recently, Rock Bottom, a short film released this past April.

Kristen and Dylan met on the set of a movie about six years ago.

Kristen told the Howard Stern Show that she lost contact with Dylan after meeting on set but ran into each other at a mutual friend’s party and she was shocked. 

“The day that I met her, it was like all bets were off,” Kristen said. 

Adding: “I met her years ago on a movie and I hadn’t seen her in six years, and then she rocked up at a friend’s birthday party, and I was like, ‘Where have you been, and how have I not known you?’ She’s been living in L.A. alongside my life somehow but not ever converging.”

The pair quickly began dating and she confessed to Howard that she told Dylan she loved her two weeks later. 

“The first time I told her I loved her, it was really late and we were in some shitty bar and her friends were there or whatever, and they walked out,” Kristen said, “and I was just like, ‘Oh, man, I’m so f**king in love with you.’ Like, done.”

Kristen seemed to have moved on fast from her breakup with model Stella Maxwell and she was photographed with Dylan in August of 2019.

She and Stella ended their relationship of two years in October 2018 but were caught sharing a smooch on a luxury yacht in July of 2019.

The actress dished on the proposal, saying: "We're marrying, we're totally gonna do it.
