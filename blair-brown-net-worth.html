Blair Brown’s Career and Net Worth

Blair Brown is an American actress with a net worth of $2 million. She has over 80 acting credits to her name, starting with the television series Police Surgeon in 1971. Brown has been nominated for two Golden Globe Awards and five Primetime Emmy Awards.

Brown graduated from the National Theatre School of Canada and acted on stage at the Stratford Shakespeare Festival. She starred in the TV mini-series Captains and Kings in 1976 and in Space in 1985. From 1987 to 1991, Brown starred in the TV series The Days and Nights of Molly Dodd. From 2008 to 2012, she starred as Nina Sharp in the television series Fringe.

Brown has also appeared in several movies, including The Paper Chase, Altered States, Space Cowboys, Dogville, and Dark Matter.
