Abby Donnelly – Professional Life

Professionally, Abby Donnelly is a young actress. She landed a starring role on the Amazon original television series Just Add Magic. It is about three girls who find a magic recipe book but don’t realize it a magic and hence get in all sorts of trouble. For this series, she played the character, Darbie. Then in 2019, she starred in the Netflix original series Malibu Rescue in the role of Lizzie. It is a story of the junior lifeguards who want to rule on the Malibu beach.

Further, her major TV debut came in 2012 when she appeared on American Horror Story. She was only 10 years old when she appeared in this series. Her movie and TV series credits include Criminal Minds, Anger Management, Suburgatory, Adam Devine’s House Party, Stumptown, and American Horror Story among others.

She is also good friends with co-star Olivia Sanabia and they are also among the two of the leading actresses on Just Add Magic. Likewise, she is also childhood friends with American actress Grace Kaufman popular for her roles in Man with a Plan, The Last Ship, and Whisker Haven Tales among many others. Similarly, one of her favorite shows is Grey’s Anatomy and her best friend inspired her to become an actress.
