Barbra Streisand's Son Shares How Her Memoir Opened His Eyes To Who His Mother Was

Publish date: 2024-07-16

Highlights

  • In an interview, Jason Gould revealed that he learned surprising things about his mom, Barbra Streisand, from her memoir My Name Is Barbra.
  • Streisand's childhood traumas, like losing her father as an infant and having an abusive stepfather, would leave her with trauma.
  • Barbra Streisand, who had a difficult relationship with her own mother, shares a close and loving bond with her son, Jason Gould.

Barbra Streisand is a mom to one child, a son, Jason Gould. The bond between mother and son is a close one, but despite their closeness, there were still things that Gould was able to learn about his mom thanks to her 2023 memoir, My Name Is Barbra.

"It was interesting because I didn't know that she was a little shoplifter as a kid," he told People in an interview on March 30, 2024. "I can't remember what she said [in detail], but I didn't know that about her." In the same interview, Gould also discussed how he was impressed with the memoir and read it all. "I thought it was very honest, and I thought it was well-written. It had painful [parts] that were a little hard to read," he said. "We all have trauma. That's the thing. Including me. And the question is, 'How do we heal it?'"

Who Is Barbra Streisand's Son Jason Gould?

In his recent interview, Jason Gould admitted to discovering some interesting details about his mother, Barbra Streisand's life. This includes the things she had to overcome and the trauma she lived with, which would, in turn, impact his own life.

"My mother lost her father when she was an infant, so that's a huge trauma," he said. "She had a stepfather who was abusive. That's a huge trauma. My father's mother had tremendous trauma. How could that not affect him and, therefore, affect me? How could my mother's trauma not, therefore, affect me? It has, even in ways that I'm sure they're not even conscious of."

But who is Gould? He was born in December 1966 and is the son of actor Elliott Gould. His parents were married for eight years but divorced in 1971. Growing up with parents who were successful in the entertainment industry, it is perhaps unsurprising that he chose to follow in their footsteps with a career as an actor and musician. However, having the confidence to perform was initially a challenge.

"I honestly didn't know if I could do it. I had never performed in front of anybody so going from zero to like 18,000 people was quite a challenge," he told People in an interview in 2017.

What Has Barbra Streisand Said About Motherhood?

Barbra Streisand had a difficult relationship with her mother and has previously discussed her coldness. "I think sometimes there are parents who don't really like themselves. They don't like their offspring either. My mother meant well. She loved me as best she could," she said, per Daily Mail. "She had dreams of her own, and she wanted to be a singer."

Streisand also commented on her mother's attitude to her success. "She never praised me to my face, but I have a feeling she praised me to other people. And she wasn't a toucher," she said. "She never hugged me or said words like, 'I love you.'" Streisand's approach to her child, Jason Gould, is quite different, and on Mother's Day in 2022, she posted a sweet photo on Facebook of them holding hands. "Nothing better than being a mother! Happy Mother's Day ladies," she captioned the post.
