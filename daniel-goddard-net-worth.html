Daniel Goddard Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-09-11

Daniel Goddard:

$16.7 million

Daniel Goddard is an Australian-American actor. He started his acting career in 1995, debuting as Eric Phillips in the Australian soap opera Home and Away. After this, he played the lead in the television series Beastmaster, playing the said role from 1999 to 2002. The following year, he made his big screen debut in the film Dream Warrior. He next had guest roles in Wild Card and Monk. In 2006, he starred in the TV movie Lightspeed. His biggest role on television was as Cane Ashby in the American soap opera The Young and the Restless, which he starred in between 2007 and 2019. At the same time, he starred in the straight-to-DVD movie Immortally Yours.
