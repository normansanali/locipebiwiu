Pokemon Go July 2023 Community Day Poliwag Rumor

Publish date: 2024-09-13

Update: Trainers, it turns out the rumors were true and Poliwag is July 2023 Community Day Pokemon.

No need to take a swim to catch this Water type—Poliwag will appear around the world during July’s #PokemonGOCommunityDay! pic.twitter.com/D8GWfaFpjY

Trainers, the seventh Community Day in Pokemon Go in 2023 is going live on July 30, 2023, and according to rumors, we might get a Poliwag Community Day.

The Community Day is a once-a-month event. During the event, a selected Community Day Pokemon appears more often, and players have a chance to catch its shiny forms. The event also brings event-exclusive bonuses, new moves, and Special Research. The sixty-seventh Pokemon Go Community Day starts on July 30, 2023, and Poliwag is the potential Community Day Pokemon. Niantic and Pokemon Go announced the dates for the next Community Days, but unfortunately, they didn’t reveal the featured Pokemon. Now, it’s up to us to start speculating what Pokemon we might see next.

A leak suggests that we might get Poliwag next. This comes from a reliable source, and we hope this turns out to be true. Now, we have a Community Day wishlist, so feel free to check it out below.
