Where Does Merry Chrysler Come From? Vine Video Meme Viral On TikTok Again

Publish date: 2024-08-25

The word Merry Chrysler comes from a famous vine video from 2015. What does the viral meme mean? Here is what we know.

Merry Chrysler girl aka Christine Sydelko is the creator of the famous vine. The hilarious video got pretty much attention in the year 2015.

It has become so well-known that we frequently see it on social media throughout the Christmas season. Read until the end to know more.

Meaning: Where Does Merry Chrysler Come From?

Merry Chrysler continues to make a comeback each holiday season. It was first released in the year 2015.

The video has a never-ending comedic value. The clip originally comes from the now-defunct short-form video app, Vine.

In the video, the creator, Christine Sydelko hilariously mispronounces ‘Christmas’. At the time, it had boasted over 323 thousand likes.

Moreover, the funny clip received over 152 thousand reviews and a massive 101 million loops.

The video went viral on Twitter, Instagram, Snapchat, and among other major social media networks.

Nonetheless, people are still using it as a funny way to wish Merry Christmas to their friends or family members.

Fans Reaction To The Vine Video Meme On TikTok

It’s been more than six years since the original Merry Chrysler Vine was posted. Social media users have reacted to it in many ways.

However, many people are still loving it as it comes out as a great reminder of the holiday season.

People are often seen using the terms: “happy chrisms”, “it’s chrismhm”, “merry crisis” or “merry chrysler” lately on Tiktok.

We all know how Tiktoker used their creativity to develop content.

A user @louthechichi has used the same theme, however, has placed a dog on her video.

Similarly, a username @rhodekok has used it as a lip-sync video to wish her friend “Merry Chrysler”.

Like this, we can find many such videos, which yield over more than a thousand results on the Tiktok search bar.

Where Is The Merry Chrysler Girl aka Christine Sydelko Now?

Merry Chrysler Girl aka Christine Sydelko is seen active on the Instagram platform.

She has obtained a verified handle on the platform with the name @csydelko. Also, has 695 thousand followers in her name.

As written in her bio, Sydelko is now a motivational speaker. She also has shared a GoFundMe fundraiser.

Sydelko had started vlogging on YouTube with fellow comedian Elijah Daniel in 2015, before going solo in 2018.

As reports suggest Sydelko had earned herself over 1.3 million YouTube subscribers by 2019.

The Vine star had also posted on her Twitter about quitting the Internet. Since then she has not been so much activity in creating vine videos.
