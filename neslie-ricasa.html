Neslie Ricasa

Publish date: 2024-09-19

Neslie Ricasa is a recognized competitive eater throughout the States. She was born on the 27th of July, 1987, in Alameda, California, USA. During her impressive career, she has been able to win many awards as well as break several records.

A name most people associate with Neslie Ricasa is that of Joey Chestnut, who was her fiance. They had participated in quite a few competitions as a couple and had also won many of them.
