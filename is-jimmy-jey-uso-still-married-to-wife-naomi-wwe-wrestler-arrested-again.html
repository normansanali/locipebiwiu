Is Jimmy Uso Still Married to his wife, Naomi? Here is everything you need to know about the WWE wrestler who is recently arrested for drunk driving. 

Jonathan Solofa Fatu, professionally known as Jimmy Uso, is an American professional wrestler who is recognized as a part of the World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). 

Known for the amazing wrestling duo, The Usos, Jimmy, along with his brother Jey Uso have many followers and fans in WWE. 

However, he does not seem to have the same reputation as he used to do previously.

And, it is because of the frequent minor crimes and arrests where he is being involved. 

Indeed, just recently, the police have arrested him for drunk driving on a very high scale than normal. 

With his sudden appearance on media, concerns regarding his personal life, relationship, and other details are increasing. 

Is Jimmy Uso Still Married To Wife Naomi?

Yes, Jimmy Uso is still married to his wife Trinity Fatu, who is professionally recognized as Naomi. 

Like Jimmy, Naomi is also a professional wrestler renowned for her incredible performances in WWE under the Raw brand. 

Jimmy and Naomi got married about 7 years ago, in the year 2014, and they have been together ever since. 

Also, Jimmy and Naomi are parents. Jimmy has 2 children, Jayla Fatu and Jaiden Fatu, with his former partner, and Naomi is their stepmother. They are all living together as a family. 

WWE Wrestler Jimmy Uso Arrested Again: Why?

The WWE star Jimmy Uso has been arrested again on a count of drink driving. 

Sources have reported that he was fought by police Blood Alcohol Concentration level of .205, which is way higher than the normal level of about .80. 

He was stopped by the police in Florida when he was going at a speed of 50 MPH on the road with a maximum limit of 35.

When stopped, he confessed that he was in possession of alcohol – beer, precisely.
