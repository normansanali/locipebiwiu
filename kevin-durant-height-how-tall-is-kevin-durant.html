Who is Kevin Durant?

Kevin Durant was born on September 29, 1988 in Washington, D.C., United States of America to Wanda Durant, his mother and  Wayne Pratt Durant his father. 

Growing up was however a little difficult for Kevin Durant who couldn’t witness the love of a United family between his parents. His parents divorced when he was at a young age. He was then helped to raise by his grandmother.
