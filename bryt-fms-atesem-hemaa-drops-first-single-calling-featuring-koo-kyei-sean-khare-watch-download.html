Bryt FMs Atesem Hemaa Drops First Single Calling Featuring Koo Kyei &amp; Sean Khare- Watch &amp; D

Publish date: 2024-09-05

Bryt FM’s news anchor Atesem Hemaa, known in real life as Grace Afumwaa, is out with her first single titled ‘Calling’.

Atesem Hemaa featured Eastern Music Awards East Side song of the year winner, Koo Kyei and Sean Khare.

It’s a classic high life which talks about a lady who has refused to answer her partner’s phone calls after she gets the impression that, her lover is cheating on her.

Atesem Hemaa, is looking at getting into the mainstream music scene under the management of Ohemaa Records alongside her radio and TV job after this song and believes ‘Calling’ will take the nation by storm.
