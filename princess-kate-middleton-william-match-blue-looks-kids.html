Kate Middleton and William Match in Blue Looks With Their Kids

Publish date: 2024-09-04

The Wales family stepped out for a fun day in Fairford, England.

Today, Prince William, Catherine, Princess of Wales, and their three kids paid a visit to the Royal International Air Tattoo, the world’s largest annual military air show, held in support of the Royal Air Force Charitable Trust.

Chris Jackson//Getty Images

For the occasion, the royals matched in preppy blue looks. Kate wore a white tee with high-waisted navy trousers with a matching leather belt. Over the top, she layered a beige blazer with white stripes, and for footwear, she opted for navy pointed-toe flats. She accessorized simply with gold hoop earrings and a dainty gold necklace.

William looked dapper in a light blue dress shirt, navy pants with a matching belt, a cobalt-blue blazer with a subtle checkered pattern in white, and brown suede boots.

Chris Jackson//Getty Images

Chris Jackson//Getty Images

The pair’s eldest, nine-year-old Prince George, broke the navy streak, instead opting for a cornflower-blue polo, teal pants, a bright blue belt, and suede brown shoes like his dad’s, paired with blue socks.

Princess Charlotte, eight, looked adorable in a blue-and-white striped dress with a Peter Pan collar, a little white cardigan, and navy slide-on sneakers. Younger brother Prince Louis, five, wore the same sneakers, his with a head-to-toe navy look consisting of a sweater layered over a white collared shirt, and long shorts.

Chris Jackson//Getty Images

Chris Jackson//Getty Images

The Prince and Princess of Wales have a strong relationship with the Royal Air Force, with the prince having served with the RAF’s Search and Rescue Force for over three years, based at RAF Valley in Anglesey. William is also the honorary air commodore of the RAF Coningsby, while Kate is the honorary air commodore of the Air Cadets.
