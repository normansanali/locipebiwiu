Kim And Kanye Celebrate Their 6 Year Anniversary... What A Difference 6 Years Makes

Publish date: 2024-09-01

Kim Kardashian and Kanye West are celebrating their 6th wedding anniversary! The couple were married in Italy on May 24th, 2014, and spent today quietly reminiscing about their incredible journey together.

What a huge difference 6 years makes! These two have gone through so many ups and downs together since they tied the knot. Their highs have been more astounding than most could imagine, but they've also suffered some pretty devastating blows as well.

One thing has remained constant for this couple so far... they've made it this far together.

Their Wedding

None of us could ever forget Kim's wedding to Kanye. Her lace dress was more luxurious than anything we could have imagined, and her guest list was more of a who's-who in Hollywood than most major awards ceremonies! It was clear right from the beginning that pairing Kim Kardashian with Kanye West would create an epic wedding celebration, and they certainly did not disappoint.

It's not just the aesthetics that were out-of-this-world. The smallest touches were executed on a grand scale. As reported by ET, when Kim Kardashian walked down the aisle, it wasn't just to regular music, it was to the sound of Andrea Bocelli singing just for her.

Their Ups And Downs Along The Way

Like any married couple, Kim and Kanye have had numerous ups and downs, but it seems that their moments happen on a much larger scale. As far as happy moments go, they're now parents to four adorable children, and they have both continued to excel in their careers. Kanye has even reached official Billionaire status.

Their challenges have been serious ones, and it seems they've been brought closer together through their struggles. Of course, the most significant challenge they've faced is moving past the horrifying robbery that Kim survived in Paris, back in 2016. Of course, Kanye stood by her side.

Here They Are Today

Their 6th anniversary, it's a bittersweet moment. As they remember their happy times they are also are very much aware of their current struggles as well. As the world faces a pandemic, Hello Magazine reports that Kim and Kanye are not out celebrating.  Home schooling has put pressure on Kim, and it's simply not the world that any of us would have imagined living in.

Nobody knows what the next 6 years will bring, but they'll surely get through it hand in hand.
