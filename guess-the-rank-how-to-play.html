Valorant Guess The Rank - What Is It And How To Play

Publish date: 2024-08-02

Guess The Rank is a popular challenge in the Valorant community, and here is what it is about and how to play it.

First Published September 20, 2023, 13:16

There are a variety of challenges in the Valorant community, and one of them is Guess The Rank, which is quite popular. Many pro teams and players, including Sentinels, and Cloud9, have also taken this challenge. If you are wondering what exactly Guess The Rank is about, here is everything you want to know.

Valorant - Guess The Rank Challenge

As the name suggests, in Guess The Rank challenge, you are shown clips of Valorant gameplays from different players out there, and you will have to guess their ranks in competitive mode. 

Professional Valorant teams usually ask their fans to record clips, and they are played in front of content creators/players, and the person who makes the most number of correct guesses wins the challenge.

If you want to play Guess The Rank, there is a dedicated website for the same where you will have to watch a total of ten clips and guess whether the player in the video is Iron, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Ascendant, ore Immortal.

If you guess the correct rank, you will get two points; however, if you guess one higher or lower rank than the correct one, you will get one point. 

You can also upload your own clips on the website, and along with this, you can compete with other players playing the game and check your rank in the leaderboard through the statistics tab under the Start Game button.

While playing, give attention to little details like the player's aim, their game sense, etc., to increase the chances of guessing the correct rank and winning the challenge.
