Is Moneybagg Yo related to Crunchy Black?

Publish date: 2024-09-03

His first mixtapes, such as From Da Block 2 Da Booth and October 20th, were released in 2012. He added three more in 2016, one of which was recognized with a Memphis Hip Hop Award for Mixtape of the Year.

Y Grizzle, Young Dolph, and OG Boo Dirty were among the special guests on Federal Reloaded, a remake of the Federal series’ debut episode. Yo Gotti and Quavo from the Migos made guest appearances on the follow-up, ELO (Everybody Lives On).

His collaboration with Yo Gotti continued on the song 2 Federal, which was released in October 2016.

Is Moneybagg Yo related to Crunchy Black?

Crunchy Black showcased a few contemporary rappers from Memphis in this VladTV Flashback from 2021. Members of the Three 6 Mafia praised Moneybagg Yo, Key Glock, Young Dolph, Yo Gotti, and Blac Youngsta.

Moneybagg was unveiled by Crunchy Black Yo, who is his cousin, who referred to him as “politically straight” since he keeps his personal affairs largely private.

Crunchy Black claimed that the positive response he receives from his fan following motivates him to be uninhibited in his interviews and public pronouncements. Check out the video clip above to hear him go into more detail.
