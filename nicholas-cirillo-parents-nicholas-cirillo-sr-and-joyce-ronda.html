Meet Nicholas Cirillo Sr and Joyce Ronda, the parents of actor Nicholas Cirillo. The actor is currently starring in Netflix’s hit show Outbanks which is currently airing its third season. The actor has rarely opened up about his family members before but we’ve done some digging up about his folks.

Who Are Nicholas Cirillo Parents?

Actor Nicholas Cirillo is the son of Joyce Ronda Cirillo and the late Nicholas Cirillo Sr. He was born on 5 June 1997 in Wilkesboro, North Carolina. Speaking with Wilkes Journal Patriot, he said at the time that he was proud to be raised in Wilkes and tries to return home as much as he can to see friends.

He said about his hometown, “I appreciate the beautiful scenery more and more every time I return.” Furthermore, he loved watching movies at the Liberty Theater in North Wilkesboro. Cirillo received acting instruction at the Winston-Salem Actor’s Group. “It’s the best training I’ve received thus far, and anything that I’ve learned really came from there,” Nicholas told the interviewer.

In the 2019 release “The Giant,” a mystery/crime thriller, he made his feature film debut as Taylor. Also, he played Luke in the hit true crime series The Act on Hulu and had a minor role in Stranger Things, another popular Netflix program.
