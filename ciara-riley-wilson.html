Ciara Riley Wilson

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Wilson in 2019, photo by kathclick/Bigstock.com

Place of Birth: Portland, Oregon, U.S.

Date of Birth: March 26, 2001

Ethnicity:
*English, German, Russian (father)
*Filipino (mother)

Ciara Riley Wilson is an American actor and dancer.

Ciara’s maternal grandfather was Hernan Leones Guiang (the son of Rodolfo Guiang and Maria Leones). Hernan was born in Bugallon, Pangasinan, the Philippines.

Ciara’s maternal grandmother was named Josefina Espino. Josefina was also born in Bugallon.

Sources: Everly Mag Interview, 2018 – https://everlymag.com
