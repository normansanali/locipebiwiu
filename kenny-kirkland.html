Biography

Kenny Kirkland was born in New York on September 28, 1955. A brilliant, spontaneous jazz musician with a legendary work ethic and a celebrated list of both jazz and non-jazz collaborations. He attended longtime friend and collaborator Branford Marsalis‘ wedding on November 7, 1998, a few days before he was found dead in his Queens apartment. He toured Europe with Polish fusion violinist Michal Urbaniak in 1977 with whom he also recorded Urbaniak from 1977 and Daybreak from 1980.
