Stephanie Courtney Wiki Biography

Stephanie Courtney, an American actress and comedian, was born on 8 February 1970 in Stony Point, New York City USA. She is most recognized for her portrayal of Flo in Progressive Insurance commercials across various media. It has been officially stated that Stephanie earns $500,000 per year from her performances in these commercials.

Stephanie has managed to accumulate a net worth of $7 million throughout her career since the mid-1990s, with most of her wealth coming from her acting, including commercials and comic shows.

Stephanie is the youngest of three siblings; their father was a history teacher, and their mother was a singer. Stephanie became interested in acting while at university, graduating from Binghamton University in 1992. During her studies, she starred as Elizabeth Proctor in “The Crucible” which further fueled her passion for acting.

After graduation, Stephanie moved to New York City, where she studied acting at the Neighbourhood Playhouse while working as an evening secretary for Robert S. Greenhill, the chairman of Smith Barney. It was during this time that Stephanie delved into advertisements, which became a major source of her net worth.

Stephanie became a member of an improvisational Los Angeles group named “The Groundlings” in 2004, which significantly contributed to her net worth.

Stephanie has appeared in over 40 TV shows and series, including “Tom Goes to the Mayor”, “Mad Men”, “Cavemen” and “Men of a Certain Age”. She has also appeared in a number of films, such as “Melvin Goes to Dinner”, “Broadcast 23”, “The Brothers Solomon”, “The Heartbreak Kid”, and “Fred: The Movie” and its sequel “Fred 2: Night of the Living Fred”. These ventures have significantly boosted her net worth.

Stephanie continues to appear in TV series such as “Major Crimes”, demonstrating her continued demand and the subsequent increase in her net worth.

In her personal life, Stephanie Courtney met Scott Kolanach in 2005 when they were both in “The Groundlings”, and the couple married in 2008.
