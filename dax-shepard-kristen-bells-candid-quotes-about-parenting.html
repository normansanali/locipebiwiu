Dax Shepard, Kristen Bells Candid Quotes About Parenting

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Kristen Bell and Dax Shepard have historically been very vocal about their ups and downs as a couple — and that openness extends to their parenting skills.

Bell and Shepard welcomed daughter Lincoln in March 2013, seven months before they tied the knot at Beverly Hills courthouse. The couple went on to welcome daughter Delta in December 2014.

While raising their back-to-back babies, the pair have been vocal about potty training, sex talks and more. The actor even had an open dialogue with his girls after his 2020 relapse following a motorcycle accident.
