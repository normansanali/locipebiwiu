Khlo Kardashian Dyes Her Hair Brown for Her Birthday

Publish date: 2024-07-24
  • Just in time for her 36th birthday, Khloé Kardashian returned to her brown hair, revealing the cosmetic change in a new Instagram post.
  • Kardashian, known for her loyalty to blonde hair dye, wrote, "Blonde still owns my heart but this brown is giving me a little something right now."
  • She also celebrated in a sparkling mesh body-con minidress.

Do brunettes have more fun? For now, Khloé Kardashian seems to think so.

The Keeping Up with the Kardashians star, known for her years-long loyalty to varying shades of blonde hair dye, recently switched things up again. She revealed her new hair color, a light brown with highlighted streaks, in a new Instagram post for her birthday.

"Birthday Glam✨Tomorrow I'm flooding my time-line with party pics," she wrote. "Btw... I'm loving my brown hair. I never thought I would say that lol blonde still owns my heart but this brown is giving me a little something right now."

In addition to showing off her cosmetic change, Kardashian's glam shots included a preview of her birthday outfit. She paired her sparkling mesh body-con minidress by designer Yousef Aljasmi with neutral undergarments, minimal jewelry, and nude pointed pumps.

Kardashian celebrated her 36th birthday at sister Kylie Jenner's house. Snaps show that Jenner decorated with pink balloons galore, letter balloons spelling out the birthday girl's nickname ("Koko"), Khloé-themed pillows, heart-shaped flower arrangements, an extravagant dessert setup, and personalized "Koko" signs and napkins. And, if we know anything about Jenner's party-planning tendencies after her annual StormiWorld festivities, the party could not have been a party without a giant inflatable slide featuring her sister's head.
