Publish date: 2024-09-20

Cobie Smulders, 28, (How I Met Your Mother airs on CBS Mondays at 8 p.m.) gets up close and personal with UsMagazine.com.

2. My character, Robin, on How I Met Your Mother became a Canadian after the creators discovered I was one and realized how awesome they are.

3. My teen celebrity crush was Zack Morris from Saved by the Bell.

4. I'm a salts, not sweets, kind of girl.

5. I speak French.

6. I always stop at lemonade stands.

7. I love America's Funniest Home Videos.

8. I've never handled a real gun, unlike Robin.

9. My father is Dutch.

11. My favorite movie is The Princess Bride.

12. I am not good at math. Not at all.

13. I was voted Most Respected in high school.

17. I'm an avid photographer.

19. I am the only one who thinks I can do a pretty good Elmo impression.

20. I want(ed) to become a marine biologist.
