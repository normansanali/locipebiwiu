Eve Torres is an American actor, dancer, model, martial arts instructor, former professional wrestler, and voice actor who is best known for her time with WWE, where she became the first-ever 3-time WWE Divas Champion. She has also been a part of several shows and movies including Skiptrace, Magic’s Biggest Secrets Finally Revealed, Attack of the Show, Sunset Tan, Show Me the Money, Matador, The Scorpion King 4: Quest for Power, The Best Damn Sports Show Period, Extreme Makeover: Weight Loss Edition, Deal or No Deal, The Soup, Supergirl, and Queens of the Ring.

    Eve Torres Facts

  • Eve Torres began her career as a dancer and model. She danced for “The Southern California Summer Pro League” in 2006.
  • Eve is the winner of the 2007 WWE Diva search.
  • On April 12, 2010, she won WWE Divas Championship and became the first WWE Diva Search winner to win the Divas Championship.
  • She became a full-time wrestler in 2009.
  • She can solve a Rubik’s cube in under 5 minutes.
  • She is fluent in English as well as Spanish.
  • She was born in Massachusetts but grew up in Denver, Colorado.
  • Eve made her video game debut in 2010 by THQ’s WWE Smackdown vs Raw 2010.
  • Eve along with Maryse and Michelle McCool has appeared on the Muscle & Fitness magazine in the January 2009 issue.
  • Her younger brother, Phillip, once participated in Are You Smarter Than a 5th Grader? and won $100,000 in October 2008.
  • Alongside Victor Webster, Ellen Hollman, Lou Ferrigno, Rutger Hauer, Royce Gracie, and Ian Whyte, she co-starred as Chancara in the 2015 direct-to-video sword and sorcery film The Scorpion King 4: Quest for Power, the 4th installment in The Scorpion King series.
  • She has appeared in various commercials and music videos.
  • She was formerly an NBA dancer.
  • In 2016, she played the character named Dasha in the Hong Kong-Chinese-American buddy cop action comedy film Skiptrace. It stars Jackie Chan, Johnny Knoxville, and Fan Bingbing.
