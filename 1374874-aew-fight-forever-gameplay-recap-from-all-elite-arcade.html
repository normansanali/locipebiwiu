AEW Fight Forever Gameplay Recap From All Elite Arcade

Publish date: 2024-09-07

The final build of AEW Fight Forever was shown off today for the first time courtesy of Evil Uno and Orange Cassidy.

On the latest episode of All Elite Arcade, All Elite Wrestling stars Orange Cassidy and Evil Uno had the honor to show off over 30 minutes of gameplay from the final build of AEW Fight Forever.

While we weren’t shown anything outside of match gameplay, four matches were shown off with some neat bits of information discovered throughout.

The four matches showcased during the stream were:

  • Hikaru Shida vs. Thunder Rosa
  • Orange Cassidy vs. Jungle Boy
  • Britt Baker vs. Chris Jericho
  • Orange Cassidy vs. MJF

Some interesting pieces of information that were discovered during the stream:

  • Jungle Boy has his “Tarzan Boy” theme by Baltimora
  • It appears Orange Cassidy has a generic theme
  • You can control your wrestler’s entrance (camera angles, pyro, etc.)
  • There is an option to “take control” following the match, which can lead to post-match beatdowns
  • Orange Cassidy can wrestle a match with his hands in his pockets

AEW Fight Forever will release on Xbox, PlayStation, Switch, and PC on Thursday, June 29.
