Leo Chenal is a 21-year-old American footballer born in Grantsburg, Wisconsin.

He currently plays professional football for the Kansas City Chiefs of the National Football League.

Leo Chenal’s wife: Is Leo Chenal married

The Kansas City linebacker who got engaged to his long-time girlfriend Randi Siebenthal earlier in January 2022 finally made it official.

He proposed to her during a vacation trip to Las Vegas. They finally tied the knot in July 2022 at a beautiful wedding ceremony attended by family and friends.

As a junior, Chenal was selected first-team All-American and honorable mention All-Big Ten Conference. As a senior, he received the Butkus-Fitzgerald Linebacker of the Year award. Chenal has 20 tackles, two tackles for loss, and one sack in 11 games during his first season.

The Wisconsin Football Coaches Association recognized Chenal as the offensive and defensive player of the year for small schools.

With 1,300 receiving yards and 17 touchdown receptions, Chenal concluded his high school career with 3,706 rushing yards and 68 touchdowns.
