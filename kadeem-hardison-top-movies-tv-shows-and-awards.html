Kadeem Hardison top movies, TV shows and awards

Publish date: 2024-09-15

Actor Kadeem Hardison was born in the United States on July 24, 1965. His most well-known performance was as Dwayne Wayne on the NBC spinoff of The Cosby Show, A Different World.

Kadeem Hardison’s top movies and TV shows

His mother, Bethann Hardison, was primarily in charge of his career. Additionally, he has appeared in several other sitcoms as a guest star, including Greek, Parenthood, The Boondocks (as himself), Living Single, Girlfriends, Under One Roof, The Boondocks, and Everybody Hates Chris as the judge.

His movies include The 6th Man, Drive, Renaissance Man, Panther, The Fantasia, School Daze, Rappin’, Def by Temptation, Vampire in Brooklyn, 30 Years to Life, and Made of Honor. The Crow: Stairway to Heaven, Bratz, Biker Boyz, Who’s Your Daddy? I’m Gonna Git You Sucka, Blind Faith, Showtime, White Men Can’t Jump, and B.C. Butcher are among the movies that Barrino’s Story: Life Is Not a Fairy Tale mentions. In the 2013 PlayStation 3 exclusive video game Beyond: Two Souls, he plays Cole Freeman as an actor.

Kadeem Hardison awards and nominations

Awards Kadeem has been able to impress both his fans and critics throughout the years, even though they have been forgiving of his amazing performances. His work on A Different World earned him two NAACP Image Awards in 1989 and 1990.

In addition, he was honored with a second NAACP Image Award for his outstanding performance in “Life is Not a Fairytale.”
