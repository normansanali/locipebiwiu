Ryan Bader Wiki Biography

Ryan DuWayne Bader, born on the 7th June 1983 in Reno, Nevada USA, is a well-known professional mixed martial artist (MMA) competing in the light heavyweight division for Bellator MMA. He gained fame as the winner of “The Ultimate Fighter: Team Nogueira Vs. Team Mir” and currently ranks third in the world in the light heavyweight category. Bader has been active in his professional career since 2007.

Have you ever wondered about Ryan Bader’s wealth as of mid-2017? According to reliable sources, his net worth is estimated to be over $5 million, accumulated through his successful career as a professional mixed martial artist.

Ryan Bader Net Worth $5 Million

Bader excelled in mixed martial arts during his time at Robert McQueen High School, securing two state championships. He then continued his training at Arizona State University, winning three Pac-10 Championship titles in 2003, 2004, and 2006, and receiving two All-American titles.

In 2007, Bader began training at Arizona Combat Sports, marking the inception of his net worth. In the following year, he participated in the 8th season of “The Ultimate Fighter”, securing three initial victories and ultimately winning The Ultimate Fighter 8 Finale. This marked the start of his successful career.

Bader continued to achieve victories in subsequent matches, greatly contributing to his net worth. Notable wins include those against notable fighters such as Antônio Rogério Nogueira, Jason Brilz, former champion Quinton Jackson, Anthony Perosh, Rafael Cavalcante, and Ovince St. Preux, contributing to his record of 18 wins and only four losses, significantly boosting his net worth. He also achieved victory in a rematch with Antônio Rogério Nogueira at UFC Fight Night 100 in 2016.

Bader signed a contract to compete for Bellator MMA in 2017, and his first match was scheduled against Muhammed Lawal at Bellator 180 on the 24th June, indicating a rising net worth. Presently, he holds 22 wins and five losses.

In his personal life, Ryan Bader has been married to Daisy since 2010, and the couple has three children together, with their current residence in Chandler, Arizona.
