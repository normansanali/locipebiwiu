Who is Rich Ting?

Rich Ting is a 4th generation Asian American actor, producer, and director.

Rich is famous for the Salt (Action Thriller) in 2010 in the role of a CIA Agent, Lone Survivor (Action Drama) in 2013 in the role of James Suh, and Warrior (TV Series) in 2019 as a Bolo.

Currently, Ting is playing the role of Carter Min in Partner Track (TV Series) which is in post-production.

Rich Ting: Career

Rich debuted as a Court reporter in Boston Legal (TV Series) in 2007. He played the role of Lenny in Beyond the Break (TV Series) in 2009.

Besides that, in the following year, he played the role of Michael in Hai Gia Dinh (TV Series).

Moreover, Ting was cast as Lee Cheol-Min in Ailiseu (TV Series) in 2013. In the next year, he played the role of Little Bang in The Novice (a TV Movie).

He made his appearance as Keith Jenloe in NCIS: Los Angeles (TV Series) in 2018.

Furthermore, Rich played the role of Captain Iijima in The Man in the High Castle (TV Series) in 2019.

In the next year, he was seen as Li Chang in Tommy (TV Series). In 2020, he played the role of Mu-jin Choi in My Name (TV Series).

As of 2022, Ting was seen as Chief of staff Seon- Moo in All of Us Are Dead (TV Series).
