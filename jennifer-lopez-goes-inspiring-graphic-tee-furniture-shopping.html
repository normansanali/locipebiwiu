See Jennifer Lopez Goe Furniture Shopping in an Inspiring Graphic Tee

Publish date: 2024-09-19

Jennifer Lopez's latest off-duty look includes an uplifting message.

Yesterday, the superstar was spotted furniture shopping LA, where she wore a white tee printed with the message "Never Too Late." She paired the top, which was also covered in tiny distressed holes, with faded wide-leg jeans and tan Timberland lace-up boots. Further accessories included a black bracelet and oversized sunglasses with a black and white rim, and her hair was styled in casual waves.

BACKGRID

The Shotgun Wedding actor has been spotted on several outings the last few months, where she wore some chic off-duty looks while shopping for her and husband Ben Affleck's new home. The ensembles have ranged from neutral to eye-catching, including a geometric maxi-length shirt dress and some '70s-inspired corduroy trousers by Chloé. On one weekday afternoon, the star elevated a black turtleneck and baggy jeans combo with designer accessories, including pointy black Christian Louboutin flats and a rare croc-embossed Hermès Birkin from her extensive collection.

Lopez has gushed over her and Affleck's relationship many times over the past few years, since the couple reunited and later married nearly twenty years after they first dated. Last November, she spoke candidly about her initial breakup with Affleck in 2004, explaining how painful it felt.

“Once we called off that wedding 20 years ago, it was the biggest heartbreak of my life. And I honestly felt like I was gonna die,” she said. “It sent me on a spiral for the next 18 years where I … couldn’t get it right. But now, 20 years later, it does have a happy ending. It has the most, would-never-happen-in-Hollywood ending. They would be like, ‘That would never happen so we’re not gonna write that because nobody would believe it’ ending.”
