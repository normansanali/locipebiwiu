Who Are Adam Levine And Krystal Levine?

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Baylen Levine might have had a discouraging school principal, but thanks to his parent’s constant support, he never gave up creating YouTube videos. Now, he stands among the top Youtube content creators.

So, who are Baylen’s parents? Keep reading to find out.

Who Are Baylen Levine Parents?

Baylen Levine was born to his parents, Adam and Krystal Levine. He said they’ve sacrificed a lot in hopes of him being a better person, and he can never repay them enough.

However, in an effort, he did surprise his dad with $40 thousand in cash — after which, the dad-son shared an emotional moment. Watch more of their “moment” in Baylen’s Youtube video titled “Surprising My Dad with $40,000!”

Likewise, he surprised his mother with a Tesla, which was also captured in a video titled “Surprising My Mom With a Tesla!”

Baylen has grabbed a great deal of success despite his young age. But his fame definitely didn’t come overnight. The Youtuber started uploading videos to his channel back in 2018, and thanks to his bold and funny prank videos, he gained a sizable number of subscribers. Eventually, his subscribers snowballed and now he has over 4.3M subscribers on his Youtube channel @BaylenLevine.

But while Baylen gained fame, his school didn’t find his pranks humorous; mainly when it was filmed on school property. Thus, he was kicked out of high school and never attended college.

Meet Adam Levine, Baylen Levine Father

Baylen Levine is named Adam Levine. No, not the Maroon 5 singer. Rather he is a “pirate” that the Youtuber looks up to the most.

“Happy Father’s Day Dad. Thank you for teaching me so much about life. Always telling the truth, surrounding myself with real people, being sober, etc you have had such an impact on me. You are one of my biggest influences. I love you,” Baylen dedicated a FB post to him.

  • Adam Levine Age

Adam Levine was born on January 27, 1971. That made him 52 years of age in 2023.

On Adam’s 52nd birthday, Baylen gifted him a Supra!

Adam is the son of Allan Neil and Vivian Levine. His father, Allan hails from Atlanta, Georgia, and is a Oil City Senior High School and Washington & Jefferson College graduate. As for his mother, Vivian is a  Chicago, Illinois native who turned 81 in 2023 — the same age as her husband.

Also, Adam has two siblings — Stewart Levine and Lori Ulibarri (Braidman)

Stewart: A DePaul University graduate who lived in  Los Angeles, California with his wife Jocelyn Chun and their beautiful baby boy.

Lori: She is a mother of three kids who attended Kofa High School, Mountain View High School, and Naperville North High School.

  • Adam Levine Job

Adam Levine owns a small business, Royal Merchant Company LLC.

Before this, he worked as a Sales Account Manager at Alcresta Therapeutics, a startup company to launch a new medical device for cystic fibrosis and exocrine pancreatic insufficient patient types.

Also, he’s been a Specialty Sales Consultant at Endo Pharmaceuticals for 5 ½ years. There, he managed the sale of a once-yearly medical implant device and an injectable in multi-state territory within Hospitals, Pediatric Endocrinology, Pediatric Surgery, and Orthopedic Surgery offices.

Furthermore, he’s worked with Astellas Pharma US as an Executive Representative Healthcare Specialist for over 7 years, and with Ortho-McNeil Pharmaceutical/ALZA Pharmaceuticals as a Professional Sales Representative for almost 3 years.

His earliest job would be as a Clinical Research Associate at Ingenix Pharmaceuticals/Worldwide Clinical Trials from April 1998 to Jan 2001. There, he ensured clinical site compliance with FDA and ICH guidelines as well as sponsor requirements; coordinated the receipt, quality control review, transmittal, and cleaning of clinical trial case reports; and, collected, entered, and reported all study tracking data such as enrollment updates and serious adverse events.

As for his education, he has a Bachelor’s degree from The University of Georgia.
